Vegan Tacos

Delicious and easy to make

Do you love tacos?

Do you also love the idea of not eating animals?

Well you have come to the right place!

  • Tortilla
  • Black beans
  • Avacado
  • Taco seasoning of your choice
  • Tofu crumble
  • lime
  • Anything else you want on it

  1. Begin by heating up the tofu crumble and black beans in a frying pan
  2. Then add in your seasoning
  3. While that is heating, cut up the avacado to the preferred size
  4. Add the ingredients to the Tortilla and then squeeze the lime over it
