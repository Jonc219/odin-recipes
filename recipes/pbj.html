Peanut Butter and Jelly

Quick and healthy

Are you short on time?

Want something tasty?

Well you have come to the right place!

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

  1. Take out two slices of bread
  2. Spread the peanut butter on each slice
  3. Then spread jelly on top of the peanut butter
  4. Combine the two slices and enjoy
